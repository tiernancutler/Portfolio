About Me

Nice to meet you :)

A California native, I moved to Florence, Italy to complete my Masters in Graphic Design, got hooked, and have called Florence home ever since.

Over the past 9 years, I’ve been a leader at a start-up tour operator. I’ve stepped into a variety of roles in order to support the company’s growing needs ranging from tour guide to graphic designer to sales manager. I’m currently the Director of both the Sales and Design departments.

When tourism came to a halt, to meet the moment in an ever-changing world and industry, I expanded my skill set to focus on user-centered design by completing year-long UX/UI certification course. On the side, I’m a semi-professional soccer player, married to a local Florentine, and am a mother to a toddler named Luca and Jack Russell named Pino.
